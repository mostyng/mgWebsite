MOSTYN GRIFFITH

ABOUT

Mostyn Griffith is a visually driven user experience designer currently based in New York City. He is looking to apply his background in visual design systems to products and experiences that create enjoyment, promote collaboration, and further access to knowledge.

In 2018, he graduated from the Rhode Island School of Design with a BFA in Graphic Design and a Minor in Computation Technology & Culture. He is currently a User Experience Designer at HUSH Studios in Brooklyn, New York. He is also 1/5 of the design studio Merl.
